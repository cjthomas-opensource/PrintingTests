This contains spools with the following clearances (and 45 deg bevels):

30 thou (0.75 mm)
25 thou (0.64 mm)
20 thou (0.5 mm)
15 thou (0.4 mm)
12 thou (0.3 mm)
10 thou (0.25 mm)

With "rough" settings, everything down to 20 thou (0.5 mm) worked as-is.
15 thou (0.4 mm) was easy to free and turns freely.
12 thou (0.3 mm) was very difficult to free and sticks when turning.
10 thou (0.25 mm) was bonded solid.
